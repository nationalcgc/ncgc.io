PREPARE TO BATTLE

Competition Structure

Phase 1

Teams must produce a working environment in accordance with specifications given, along with a report that provides a detailed and comprehensive outline of the environment and its workings.

Phase 2

Competitors will be randomly assigned to another team's environment. Their goal is to harden the new system as much as possible before the explotation rounds.

Phase 3

Systems will again be randomized to teams. In this phase, competitors race against the clock to exploit and backdoor the system.

Phase 4

In the final round, teams will get their last environment randomly assigned. The competition firewall will be dropped this round. Teams will not only have to defend their systems, but exploit the other systems.

Ten teams. Four challenges. Three days.
